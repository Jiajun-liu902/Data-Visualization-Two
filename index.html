Population Density of Countries in different years

Dear tourists,

Welcome to visit our data visualization portfolio. Now, we are going to analysis the growing trend of the global population during recent years. The growth of the population could not only provide sufficient labour force in each country for industry and manufacturing, it also have a great impact on the economic growth and sustainable human development.

Firstly, this choropleth map used different brightness of the colour to encode the population density of each country. It is important to note that the population density was relied on the local climate, terrain and environment of each country. In addition, sometimes, it also showed the correlation between certain country and its neighbourhoods. After that, the filter indicated the population density in different years.

Secondly, the bar chart has illustrated the population density in different continents. It clearly showed that which continent is more suitable for human to living in.
Finally, in appendix, it has one addition pie chart and it clearly showed the percentage of each continent population among the whole world.

In conclusion, the growth of the population is not only reducing the living space per capita, but also make some challenges to the sustainable development of the economic and environment. Due to the extractive resources is reducing throughout the years.
